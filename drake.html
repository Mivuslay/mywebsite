Drake

Honestly Nevermind, is the seventh studio album by Canadian rapper and singer Drake, which was surprise release on June 17, 2022. Honestly, Nevermind is a dance album driven by house and Baltimore club influences. The album includes elements of Jersey Club, hard techno, R&B, Amapiano, Ballroom, and Drake's traditional style of hip-hop .
