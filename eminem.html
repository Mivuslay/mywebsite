Eminem

Curtain Call, is the first greatest hits album by American rapper Eminem. It was released on December 6, 2005. A live version of "Stan" performed with English singer-songwriter Elton John at the 43rd Grammy Awards. The album was certified Diamond in the United States on March 8, 2022, and quintuple platinum in New Zealand. It reached number one on several charts, including the US Billboard 200 and UK Albums Chart.
